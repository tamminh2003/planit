Question

How do I configure the Netgear Nighthawk M2 Mobile broadband device?

 Answer

The Netgear Nighthawk M2 Mobile Broadband device will come in a box containing the following

  • • Nighthawk M2 Mobile Router and battery
  • • Quick start guide
  • • Manufacturer’s warranty card
  • • USB Type-C cable
  • • AC adapter

The modem comes with the following controls

  • Power button
  • Home button
  • Return (Go back) button
  • Touch Screen

 

 

  1. Connect the device to power in a location near to your working area. The device comes fully charged but to ensure a stable connection it is recommended to connect the device to mains power.

  2. Press and hold the Power button for three seconds or until the setup screen appears.

  3. Press Lets go

  4. Press Customize.

  5. Press Create admin password.

  6. Create a new password and press Done.

    Note: Information Services recommends you do not use your La Trobe Password.

  7. Press Go to my dashboard.

    Note: Please see Mobile broadband devices for information relating to your data allowance.

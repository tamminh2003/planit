Overview

The Wi-Fi only iPad is the defined standard for the University. You should use free Wi-Fi where possible or whenever available for related university activities.


Wi-Fi only iPad

The WiFi iPad device is the defined standard for the University. Access to the network on this device is via Wi-Fi only.

Wi-Fi + Cellular iPad

If there is requirement for a non-standard iPad cellular device where related university activities exists for off campus and/or out of business hours use.  A detailed justification will need to be provided with a compelling business requirement endorsed by the line manager and financial approver.  A nominated Senior Executive Group will review the justification and advise accordingly of appproval or non-approval.

In the instance where approval is granted for a  (Wi-Fi + Cellular) iPad.  Please note that a SIM would be required and monthly charges would be passed back to the department.

Can I choose the colour of my iPad?

There is no specific color specification, the vendor will determine the best option for timely delivery.

How do I order an iPad?

To order an iPad, please complete the New or Replacement Mobile Device request under Telephony & Mobile on ICT Service Catalogue.

Note: Mobile device purchases require manager approval.  Model required can be requested via the comments field within the mobile device order form.
